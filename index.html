End-to-end Fashion Analytics Project

Vibe clustering, pricing insights & brand positioning

A full analytics pipeline on SSENSE product listings: cleaning ~9k products, embedding product descriptions, discovering 8 aesthetic “Vibes”, and building a Power BI dashboard to tell the story.

  • Used sentence-transformer embeddings and K-Means to discover 8 fashion vibes
  • Mapped brands into a vibe space and analysed price vs vibe behaviour
  • Designed a luxury-styled Power BI dashboard for non-technical stakeholders
